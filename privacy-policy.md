Privacy Policy

This Facebook app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. 
It only performs automated posting to the Facebook Page authorized by the page administrator.

The app does not access user data beyond what is required for the 'pages_manage_posts' 
and 'pages_read_engagement' permissions.

No personal data is saved on external servers.  
No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

If you have questions, contact the Page Administrator directly through Facebook.
